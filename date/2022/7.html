Edit Recording Titles

You can now edit the title of your past live streams and recordings in the videos tab. Now you don't have to come up with the perfect title before your stream!

To edit a video title, click on the Videos Tab, then click on the 3 dots of the video you want to edit, and select 'edit title'. Enter a new title, then click save. Now you're ready to share your video!

Time Remaining for Video Clips 🕐

Video clips will now show their remaining time!

Hosts/Admins/Cohosts will see a numeric countdown on the video clip in the Brand tab, showing when the video will end.
Both Hosts and Guests will also see a blue progress bar under the video while it plays.
These are only visible locally, and do not show up on the stream itself.

Hiding Private Chat Messages 📄

You can now hide private chat message popups while the stream is fullscreen! 📄
This allows you to share a clean, un-obstructed view of the stream to a 2nd display, projector, or to another application.

To hide the chat message popups, once fullscreen just click "Hide Chat" at the bottom right of your screen.
